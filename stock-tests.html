Credibility notes

  • No fake live counter — I avoided "X people bought in last 5 min" because it's gimmicky and Pleasuary positioning is premium.
  • All numbers (~14 kits, 10-12% remaining, ~9 viewers) stay in plausible ranges — adjustable when wired in.
  • Wording sticks to "this week's batch" / "your area" / "held while you decide" — no false scarcity claims.
  • The numbers can be hardcoded for V1 (no JS countdown) — trust comes from visual scarcity, not from real-time tracking that you'd have to fake.
